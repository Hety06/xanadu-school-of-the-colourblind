Information Station

Guest Speaker

Be sure to join us Friday, March 24th at 6:30 in the auditorium to listen to guest speaker and traveler Antoni Barkdain. He will be talking about his many travels all across the globe and how he has dealt with colourblindness through it all. He will also discuss opportunities for studying and working abroad, so refreshments will be provided so people actually show up.

Lunch Seminar

Anybody interested in going to our annual Lunch Seminar regarding teachers eating the students' lunches needs to sign up in the library. We will be focusing this year on our main point, "They're Dogs, They Can't Help It". The seminar is free, but bringing a bag of kibble is appreciated.

School Art Project

Our school art project theme for this year is Under the Sea and will be Saturday, May 20th. Those who struggle with differentiating between green, blue, and purple are more than welcome to help out. We will be needing donated items such as old t-shirts, paints in more than just grey, brushes, and old glass jars and cups. To sign up or to bring items in, talk to Ms. Verditer in room 578.
